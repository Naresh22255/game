Snakes and Ladders

    Choose two players to start the game.

    Once you have selected the players hit start at the bottom of the page to start the game.

    Each Player gets a chance to roll the dice and if the player has roled six, he will get one more chance to roll.

    The green line is considered as ladder and the red line is considered as snake.

    The player who reaches 50 first is considered as winner.
